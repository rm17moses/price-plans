About Moses Mobile Networks

Moses Mobile Networks is a leading mobile network operator in South Africa, providing voice, data, and digital services to over 20 million customers across the country. We are committed to delivering innovative and affordable solutions that connect people and enrich their lives.

Our Vision

To be the most loved and trusted mobile network in South Africa.

Our Mission

To empower our customers with the best network experience, products, and services that suit their needs and lifestyles.

Our Values

  • Customer-centricity: We put our customers first in everything we do.
  • Excellence: We strive for excellence in our performance and quality.
  • Innovation: We embrace innovation and creativity to deliver value-added solutions.
  • Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Teamwork: We work together as one team to achieve our goals.

Our Services

We offer a range of services to cater to different customer segments and needs, including:

  • Prepaid and postpaid voice and data plans
  • Mobile broadband and Wi-Fi services
  • Digital content and entertainment platforms
  • Mobile money and financial services
  • Enterprise solutions and cloud services
  • Retail stores and online channels

Our Achievements

We are proud of our achievements as a mobile network operator in South Africa, such as:

  • Having the fastest and most reliable 5G network in the country
  • Winning multiple awards for customer satisfaction and service excellence
  • Supporting social responsibility initiatives in education, health, and environment
  • Partnering with global technology leaders to bring cutting-edge solutions to our customers
